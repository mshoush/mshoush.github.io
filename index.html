About

I'm a Data & Applied AI Researcher with a PhD in Computer Science and over 5 years of experience in socio-technical AI research. My work lies at the intersection of Information Systems and Applied AI, focusing on how AI and digital business data can be used to understand and responsibly prescribe improvements to business workflows, routines, and roles. I have led industry-collaborative projects and am passionate about teaching Information Systems, AI, and Business Process Mining at undergraduate and graduate levels. My research interests include prescriptive process monitoring, reinforcement learning, trustworthy AI (synthetic data, privacy, fairness), causal inference, and explainable AI. I aim to translate advanced AI methods into applied, high-impact solutions that improve organizational performance.
