Kairos: AI-driven prescriptive monitoring

Kairos is a web-based tool that provides actionable recommendations for ongoing business process cases. It uses reinforcement learning to suggest interventions that prevent undesirable outcomes (e.g., late deliveries, high costs) under uncertainty and resource constraints. The tool was validated on real-world event logs and received the Best Demo Paper Award at the International Conference on Business Process Management (BPM) 2023.

The system integrates with standard process mining formats (XES) and supports explainable AI features (Shapley values) to justify recommendations. It is designed to be extensible for different business KPIs and intervention types.
